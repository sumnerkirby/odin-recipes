No Bake Cookies

Description

No bake cookies are an excellent, easy recipe that require just a few simple ingredients. However, tempering the chocolate just right is a tempermental process to be sure.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1/2 cup peanutbutter

Steps

  1. Bring sugar, margarine, milk, cocoa, and salt to a rapid boil in a saucepan for 1 minute.
  2. Add quick-cooking oats, peanut butter, and vanilla; mix well.
  3. Working quickly, drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper and let cool.
  4. Enjoy!
